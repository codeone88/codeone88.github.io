Welcome to our creator community!

LoFiU is an app dedicated to creators, providing a platform to share and monetize your unique content. Whether you’re crafting soothing lofi visuals, relaxing music, or creative artwork, LoFiU empowers you to reach a passionate audience while earning from your work.
Before getting started, please review and accept the following policies:

1. Content Guidelines

• Free & Paid Content:
You can upload both free and paid content to share with your audience.

• Image Quality:
All image uploads must be in 4K resolution to ensure the best user experience.

• Original Work:
Only share content you own or have the rights to distribute. Copyright violations are not allowed and may lead to account suspension.

2. Earnings & Payments

• In-App Currency:
Paid content is purchased using coins inside the app. Users can buy 100 coins for $4.99 USD.

• Revenue Share:
You’ll receive 60% of the revenue from every sale. This split covers app store fees and transaction costs.

• Payouts:
• Payments are processed via PayPal once you reach $10 USD (340 coins) in earnings.
• Need an alternative payment method? Contact us to discuss options.
• Earnings Tracking: Every sale is logged in our database to track your income accurately. You can monitor your earnings in your dashboard. (coming soon)

3. Payment Schedule

• Payouts are processed on a monthly basis once you reach the minimum threshold.
• Ensure your PayPal information is correct to avoid payment delays.

4. Community Standards

• Respect our community by sharing high-quality, appropriate content.
• Inappropriate or offensive content is strictly prohibited.

5. Support & Contact

• For any questions or to discuss alternative payment methods, reach out to our support team directly through our links.
